# Why AI Voice Is Mostly Telephony

Before working with AI voice systems, I expected the biggest challenges to be AI-related.

What surprised me was how much traditional VoIP knowledge still matters.

SIP is still SIP.

RTP is still RTP.

NAT is still NAT.

Codecs are still codecs.

The only new participant in the conversation is the AI.

Many AI voice problems are actually telephony problems:

- SIP routing
- media flow
- network connectivity
- firewall behavior
- carrier interoperability

AI voice does not replace telephony fundamentals.

It builds on top of them.
