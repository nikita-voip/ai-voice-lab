# Building a Real-Time Voice Translator

As part of my AI Voice learning journey, I built a simple real-time voice translation prototype using Vapi.

The goal was straightforward:

Person A speaks one language.

Person B speaks another language.

The system translates the conversation in real time.

Areas tested:

- Voice recognition
- Language detection
- AI processing
- Speech synthesis
- Conversation flow

What surprised me most was not the technology itself.

The technology already works.

The real challenges are:

- latency
- conversation timing
- interruptions
- user experience
- operational costs

The future of voice communication is becoming increasingly language-independent.

Real-time translation is no longer science fiction.
