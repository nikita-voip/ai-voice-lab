# OpenAI Realtime Notes

While experimenting with AI Voice systems, I spent time testing OpenAI Realtime capabilities.

What stood out:

- low latency responses
- natural conversation flow
- interrupt handling
- streaming audio processing

The technology feels very different from traditional chatbot interactions.

The challenge is not generating responses.

The challenge is integrating real-time AI into telephony environments.

Key lesson:

Realtime AI becomes significantly more valuable when connected to real communication systems.
