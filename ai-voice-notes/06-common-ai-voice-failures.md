# Common AI Voice Failures

Most AI voice problems are not caused by the AI model itself.

Common failure points include:

- SIP registration failures
- RTP issues
- NAT problems
- WebSocket disconnects
- STT errors
- TTS failures
- API timeouts

In many cases, troubleshooting AI Voice systems looks very similar to troubleshooting traditional VoIP systems.

The technology stack is different.

The troubleshooting mindset remains the same.
