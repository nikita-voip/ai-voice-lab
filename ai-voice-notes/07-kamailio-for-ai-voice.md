# Kamailio For AI Voice Projects

During AI Voice research, I spent time exploring Kamailio-based architectures.

Many engineers view Kamailio only as an SBC.

In practice, it can serve many different roles:

- SIP router
- traffic distribution
- scalability layer
- session management
- integration point

For large AI Voice deployments, architecture becomes just as important as the AI itself.

The AI may answer the call.

The surrounding infrastructure keeps the platform running.
