# FreeSWITCH Conference Notes

One area I explored was conference architecture using FreeSWITCH.

Interesting capabilities include:

- conference rooms
- participant roles
- moderation
- recording
- event control

Conference systems become particularly interesting when combined with AI services.

Examples:

- real-time transcription
- voice translation
- AI moderation
- conversation analysis

The combination of FreeSWITCH and AI creates opportunities far beyond traditional conferencing.
